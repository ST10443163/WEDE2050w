Our Story

Healing Tails was created on a simple belief that animals have a healing effect on people and vice versa. We believe that animals and people who overcome their fears and trauma together build an unbreakable bond and live a much happier life.

The idea originated when we realized that trauma survivors who had adopted rescue animals noticed a massive shift in their wellbeing. We believe that the companionship of a loyal dog, the gentle presence of a cat or the playful nature of a rabbit or bird offers a different level of comfort that traditional therapy fails to achieve.

Our Purpose

We strengthen bonds between rescue animals and humans through mutual healing. We provide programs that pair animals with individuals to create a safe environment for them both and build community projects that spread love, kindness and understanding.

Every step we take is guided by the belief that healing is not one sided. When we care for animals, they care for us in return.

Our Dream

We dream of a world where every animal has a home and every person has access to a healing tail. We want communities to embrace the value of kindness, empathy and connection.

We believe that when shelters are empty, hearts are full. Every wag, purr, squeak and flutter of wings brings hope. We strive to show that animals and humans can create a healthier, happier and more compassionate world together.
