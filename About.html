Our story

Healing Tails was created on a simple belief that animals have a healing effect on people and vise versa. We belive that animals and people who overcome their fears and trauma together build an unbreakable bond and live a much happier life.

The idea originated when we realized that when trauma survivors, who had adopted a rescue animal, noticed a massive shift in their wellbeing. We believe that the companionship of a loyal dog, the gentle presense of a cat or the playful nature of a rabbit or bird offers a diffirent level of comfort that traditional therapy fails to achieve. After hearing all these stories about trauma survivors, we decided to create Healing Tails to formally introduce rescued animals and individuals in need of emotional, mental and physical support.

Over time Healing Tails have grown from a small community initiative into a well structured non-profit organization. Today it serves as a safe space where both humans and animals can find trust, companionship and healing.

Each rescue story becomes a part of Healing Tail - of HOPE.

We thrive to by giving animals onother chance at life while also giving survivors the gift of unconditional love.


Our purpose

We strengthen bonds between rescue animals and humans through mutual healing. We provide programs that pair animals with individuals to create a safe environment for them both and build community projects that spreads love, kindness and understanding. Every step we take is guided by the belief that healing is not one sided.

When we care for animals, they care for us in return.


Our dream

We dream of a world where every animal have a home and every person have access to a healing tail. We want communities to embrace the value of kindness, empathy and connection.

We believe that when shelters are empty, hearts are full. Every wag, purr, squeack and flutte of wings brings hope. We strive in showing that animals and humans can create a healthier, happier and more compassionate world together.
