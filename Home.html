About Us

Healing Tails connects rescued animals with humans in emotional recovery, creating bonds that heal hearts on both ends of the leash. Our programs bring comfort, companionship, and hope to people who need it most, while giving animals a second chance at a loving life.

Our Programs

  • Animal-Assisted Therapy Visits
  • One-on-One Companion Pairing
  • Community Healing Events
  • Rescue & Rehabilitation Partnerships
