Get involves with healing tails

At Healing Tails, every helping hand and paw makes a difference, whether you have time, skills, resources or simply a big heart. We offer many different ways to be a part of our journey of healing and hope.


Ways you can help

Volunteer
  • Walk, socialize and play with rescued animals
  • Assist in therapy programs qith survivors
  • Help at events and fundraisers
Foster or adopt
  • Provide a safe temporary home
  • Give a forever home to an animal in need
Donate
  • One time or monthly donations
  • Sponsor a animal or therapy session
  • In kind donations (Food, toys, blankets, ect)
Partnerships & sponsorships
  • Collaborate as a business or organization
  • Sponsor a program, event or therapy dog
Spread the word
  • Follow and share us on social media
  • Invite friends and family to join
  • Host small community awereness events
