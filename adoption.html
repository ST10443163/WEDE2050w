Find Your Healing Companion

Every animal at Healing Tails has a story of resilience and hope. When you adopt from us, you're not just giving an animal a home - you're welcoming a healing companion who understands struggle and knows the power of love and connection.

Our adoption process ensures the perfect match between animal and human, creating bonds that heal both hearts. Browse our available animals below and find your perfect match.

Our Adoption Process

1

Application

Fill out our adoption application form with your information and preferences.

2

Interview

We'll schedule a phone or in-person interview to discuss your application.

3

Home Visit

For some animals, we conduct a home visit to ensure a safe environment.

4

Meet & Greet

You'll meet your potential new companion to ensure it's a good match.

5

Adoption

Complete the paperwork and welcome your new healing companion home!
