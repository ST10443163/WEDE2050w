Our Programs

We believe that every bond between animals and humans is unique and so is every healing journey. That is why we offer a variety of programs designed to bring comfort, companionship and growth to people of all ages and backgrounds.

Companion Healing Program

We match rescued dogs, cats, rabbits and birds with individuals facing emotional struggles, trauma recovery or loneliness. We train our animals to be exactly what each individual needs and to provide unconditional love while people gain a safe space to heal and grow.

Therapy & Support Sessions

We provide therapy sessions, accompanied by our certified therapy animals, for trauma survivors, children with special needs and individuals in recovery. Our animals provide gentle interaction that reduces stress and creates lasting comfort.

Youth & School Outreach

We partner with schools and youth centers to teach empathy, kindness and responsibility through structured activities with animals. This program allows children and young adults to build confidence, reduce anxiety and learn valuable life skills.

Community Events & Healing Circles

From group workshops to outdoor healing with animals days, we bring people together to experience the joy of connecting with animals. These events create community, reduce isolation and spread awareness about animal welfare.

Animal Assisted Wellness Activities

We provide guided walks, mindfulness with animals and play therapy sessions. This helps participants find peace, grounding and joy in the presence of their furry or feathered friend.

Healing Tails is a small non-profit organization.
Together we are creating a world where every tail heals a heart.
