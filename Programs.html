Our programs

We believe that every bond between animals and humans are unique and so is every healing journey. That is why we offer a variety of programs designed to bring comfort, companionship and growth to people of all ages and backgrounds.


Companion healing program

We match rescued dogs, cats, rabbits and birds with individuals facing emotional struggles, trauma recovery or loneliness. We train our animals to be exactly what each individual needs and to provide unconditional love while people gain a safe space to heal and grow.


Therapy & support sessions

We provide therapy sessions, acompanied by our Certified therapy animals, for trauma survivors, children with special needs and individuals in recovery. Our animals provide a gentle interaction that reduces stress and create lasting comfort.
